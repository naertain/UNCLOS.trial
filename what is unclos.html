MEANING

Agreement that establish the legal framework for marine and maritine activities also replace the sea of freedom concept. Its establish in November 16 1982, but the proposal first made in 1973 and is presently binding for 154 states

ARTICLES & ANNEXES

THE BORDER AND HOW IT WORKS

  1. BASELINE

    2. The low water and it count from the coast. All the water behind the baseline is internal water and theres no foreign vessel of anytype allowed to pass

  2. TERETORIAL SEA

    3. It length 12 NM (Nautical Mile) from the coast. The coastal state have rights to exploit the resource, and no foreign ship allowed unless theyre innocent (they're motive isn't about to exploit the resource,spying and doesn't bring any weapon)

  3. CONTIGOUS ZONE

    4. Its 24 NM from the coast. foreign vessel allowed to pass but they're innocent and coastal state may make a rule to prevent infringment of it custom and allowed to arrest the vessel that infringe the law

  4. EXCULUSSIVE ECONOMIC ZONE

    5. Its 200 NM from the coast. Contribute to economy of the coastal state. foreign vessel allowed to lay cable, trade, sail ensuring they're not harming the sea state coastal state has sovereign rights for purpose, exploration, usage, and management of natural resource

  5. CONTINENTAL SHELF

    6. Its 350 NM from the coast. the coastal state are only allowed to exploit the resource that attached from the ocean bed
